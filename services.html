    24 HOUR AMBULANCE SERVICE

    We take pride in our ambulance fleet and we offer Advanced Life Support and Basic Life Support ambulances based on corporate contracts and for general clientele. We are operational 24/7 including public holidays and we work in association with other ambulance service providers within and outside Nairobi to ensure prompt and high quality service delivery.

    The vehicles are fully equipped with the following; Emergency medicines, State of the art emergency equipment such as cardiac monitors, ventilators, defibrillators, suctions equipment among others. All ready to save lives before patients arrive at the hospital. The institution’s critical care ambulances have also been fitted with full patient life support during transportation from the site to the hospital. In addition to the above evacuation services, the hospital also facilitates and makes arrangements for air ambulance. Patients’ lives come first and to ensure they are well taken care of during evacuation, there is always a team of highly trained staff comprising of emergency medical technicians, emergency nurses and doctors.

    HOME CARE SERVICE

    Home care can be the key to achieving the highest quality of life possible. It can enable safety, security, and increased independence; it can ease management of an ongoing medical condition; it can help avoid unnecessary hospitalization; it can aid with recovery after an illness, injury, or hospital stay—all through care given in the comfort and familiarity of home. Home care can include: Help with daily activities such as dressing and bathing Assistance with safely managing tasks around the house Companionship Therapy and rehabilitative services Short- or long-term nursing care for an illness, disease, or disability—including tracheostomy and ventilator care

    Home Health Care services may include:
    • Short-term nursing services
    • Physical therapy
    • Occupational therapy
    • Speech language pathology​
    • Medical social work
    • Home health aide services​

    BED FACILITY

    Our hospital provides comprehensive care for unstable and critically ill patients in urgent need of advanced monitoring and intensive care treatment. These services are carried out in precision and coordination of a team of expert doctors. We have a bed capacity of 1,000 inclusive of ICU and isolation rooms with 24 hour doctor coverage. Each of the beds in the ICU and isolation rooms have a state of the art technology that enables staff to monitor patient’s vital signs continually.
